# Getting Started

## Prerequisites

- A modern web browser
- An invitation code (if required by your administrator)

## Registration

1. Navigate to the login page.
2. Click "Register".
3. Fill in your details.
4. Verify your email (if enabled).

## First Steps

Once logged in, you can:
- **Complete your profile**: Add an avatar and banner.
- **Explore the Knowledge Base**: Create your first note.
- **Join a Family**: Ask your family admin for an invite link.
