# Features

## Knowledge Base
Store your thoughts, documents, and links in a structured way. Use tags and categories to organize your content.

## Family Hub
Manage your family's digital life. Share calendars, lists, and important documents securely.

## Digital Shop
Browse the shop for themes, plugins, and other digital assets to enhance your Second Brain experience.

## University Clubs
If enabled, join and manage university clubs, view events, and connect with other members.
