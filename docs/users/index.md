# User Guide

Welcome to the User Guide! This section is designed to help you get the most out of the Second Brain Database.

## What you can do

- **Manage Your Profile**: Customize your avatar, banner, and theme.
- **Organize Knowledge**: Create and manage your personal knowledge base.
- **Family Hub**: Connect with family members and share resources.
- **Shop**: Browse and purchase digital assets.

## Getting Started

If you are new here, we recommend starting with the [Getting Started](getting-started.md) guide.
